SciLifeLab Serve (beta) is developed and operated by the SciLifeLab Data Centre. SciLifeLab Serve is free to use for all life science researchers affiliated with a Swedish research institution and their collaborators. The service is based on an open source platform working on top of a Kubernetes cluster.

Please email serve@scilifelab.se with any questions.
