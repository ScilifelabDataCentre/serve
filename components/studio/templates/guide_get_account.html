Access to SciLifeLab Serve is granted for:

  • Life science researchers affiliated with a Swedish university.
  • A collaborator of someone affiliated with a Swedish research institute.
  • SciLifeLab infrastructure units.
  • Others under specific circumstances.

To access SciLifeLab Serve, you need one of the following:

  • A Life Science Login (LS-Login).
  • A manually created account (for those unable to acquire an LS login).

Setting up a new LS-Login

To set up a new LS-Login, follow the steps carefully. When creating an LS-Login, you will also acquire an ELIXIR membership.

  1. Start by clicking the green 'Sign in' button in the top right corner of this page.
  2. After redirection, click on the 'Life Science Login (university)' button.
  3. Type the name of your institution in the 'Choose Your Identity Provider' box and choose the correct option.
  4. Use your institutional credentials (SWAMID) to log in when redirected.
  5. Click 'Proceed to register on the Life Science Login'.
  6. Read and confirm the user and data policies, then complete the registration form and click Submit.
  7. Now you should have received a verification email. Check your email inbox and click the verification link/button in the received email.
  8. If you have not done so previously, you will be asked to register with ELIXIR. Click 'Submit' after completing the registration form.
  9. Click 'Continue' on the subsequent page indicating that you are a member of ELIXIR.
  10. Double check the summarized information. Click 'Consent' to proceed.
  11. You will now be redirected to your SciLifeLab Serve account. For help creating a project, see the Projects guide.

Troubleshooting
